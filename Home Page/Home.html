Zen Academy Facility

Class Room

Classrooms and lecture halls are adorned with intricate carvings and tapestries depicting legendary battles and heroic deeds, providing an immersive backdrop for the study of dragon lore, history. Skilled instructors, themselves seasoned dragon riders and warriors, impart their wisdom to eager pupils, teaching them the art of dragon training, the secrets of ancient runes, and the techniques of combat and diplomacy.

Training Ground

The training ground is divided into distinct areas, each tailored to accommodate the unique needs of both Vikings and dragons. For aspiring dragon riders, there are spacious paddocks and stables where they can bond with their majestic steeds, learning to communicate through word and gesture, and mastering the art of riding with grace and skill.

Dinning Room

Entering the Viking Hall, visitors are greeted by the sight of soaring arches and intricately carved pillars, adorned with symbols of strength, wisdom, and valor. The air is filled with the scent of burning pine and fragrant herbs, evoking memories of ancient rituals and ancestral homesteads.
