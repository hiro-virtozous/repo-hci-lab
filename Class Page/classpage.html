Wind Whisperers

"Wind Whisperers" is a class specifically designed for dragons who understand the power and grace of the wind. They are agile inhabitants of the sky, harnessing the wind with gentleness and precision.

Dragons who follow the path of Wind Whisperers develop a strong bond with the element of wind, understanding its subtle yet powerful flows and movements. They learn to harness the power of the wind to traverse the skies with astonishing speed and to use it to their advantage in battles.

Flameforge Masters

"Flameforge Masters" is a class specifically tailored for dragons who embrace the power and might of the blazing fire. They are the masters of the fire element, wielding the ability to generate and control flames with remarkable precision and grace.

Dragons who choose the path of Flameforge Masters undergo rigorous training and deep meditation, fostering a spiritual bond with the engulfing element of fire. They comprehend the flow and potency of fire in all its forms, from roaring flames to smoldering embers.

Luminance Wardens

"Luminance Wardens" is a unique class tailored for dragons who have embraced the fiery power of light. They are the guardians of light, bringing the warmth and wisdom of the sun as well as the radiant power of the blazing fire.

Dragons who embark on the path of Luminance Wardens have chosen to become protectors against darkness, bringing light in its purest and most enchanting form. They learn to control the energy of light with finesse and precision, understanding how to channel it to bring goodness to those in need and to repel the evil that lurks in the shadows.

Dark Magisters

"Dark Magisters" is a class specifically tailored for dragons who have tapped into the hidden power of darkness within their realm. They are masters of the dark arts, wielding black magic with intelligence and sinister grace.

Dragons who tread the path of Dark Magisters have ventured into the realm of mystery and danger, exploring the streams of dark energy that course through their world. They delve into hidden knowledge of curses, sorcery, and other forbidden powers, honing their abilities to manipulate and control darkness itself.

Lightning Reavers

"Lightning Reavers" is a class specifically crafted for dragons who command the formidable power of lightning. They are unrivaled hunters of the sky, wielding lightning bolts to devastate enemies and dominate the battlefield.

Dragons who choose the path of Lightning Reavers undergo intensive training and develop a profound bond with the element of lightning. They learn to summon and direct lightning bolts with deadly precision, harnessing the power of nature to bolster their attacks.
